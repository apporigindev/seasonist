Seasonist — Privacy Policy

In short: we collect no personal data

Seasonist is a personal color analysis tool that runs entirely on your own device, inside your browser. It has no server, no user accounts, no database and no analytics. Your photo is analysed locally on your device and is never uploaded, transmitted, stored or shared. We do not collect, receive or hold any personal data about you.

Because no personal data ever reaches us, App-Origin is not a "data controller" (администратор на лични данни) of any personal data concerning you within the meaning of Regulation (EU) 2016/679 (GDPR) — there is simply no personal data for us to control. This policy explains, honestly and in plain language, how the app works and why that is true.

How your photo is processed

To read your undertone, depth and contrast, Seasonist asks for one daylight photo — taken with your camera or chosen from your gallery.

That photo is processed only in your device's memory, by code running in your browser. A face-detection model (Google's MediaPipe Face Landmarker) locates points on your face so the app can sample a few skin, eye and hair colours and map them to a season. All of this happens on your device.

Your photo is never sent to us or to anyone else. It is not uploaded to any server, not saved to any database, and not used to train any model. When you close or refresh the page, retake the photo, or tap "Delete my analysis", the photo and the result disappear from memory. There is nothing left for us to keep, because we never received anything.

What is stored on your device

So that the app can work offline and load quickly, your browser keeps a local cache on your device. This cache contains only the app's own files (its code, styles and icons) and the face-detection model and runtime, downloaded once and reused. None of this is personal data — it is the same for every user and says nothing about you.

The app may also keep a small, non-identifying setting on your device (for example a display preference such as your chosen language). Such a setting is a technical preference, not personal data, and does not identify you.

Everything stored on the device stays on the device. You are always in control: you can remove all of it at any time by clearing this site's data (or the app's data) in your browser or device settings, or by uninstalling the app. Clearing it will not delete anything of yours from anywhere else, because there is nowhere else — nothing was ever uploaded.

Third-party resources the app loads

To run, the app loads a few technical resources from external services. Like any request your browser makes to a website, these connections are visible to the service you connect to and inherently reveal basic technical request data such as your IP address, the time of the request and general device or browser information. That is a normal part of how the web works, and it is not something we collect or receive — we operate no server in this exchange.

Crucially, none of these requests contains your photo, your analysis result or any personal data about you. After the first run the resources are served from your device's cache, so most later visits make no such request at all.

The external resources are:
• Google Fonts (fonts.googleapis.com, fonts.gstatic.com) — the app's typefaces. Google privacy policy: https://policies.google.com/privacy
• jsDelivr CDN (cdn.jsdelivr.net) — the MediaPipe runtime and its WebAssembly files. jsDelivr privacy policy: https://www.jsdelivr.com/terms/privacy-policy-jsdelivr-net
• Google-hosted MediaPipe storage (storage.googleapis.com) — the face-detection model (~3 MB). Google privacy policy: https://policies.google.com/privacy

These providers are independent operators of their own services; how they handle the technical request data described above is governed by their own privacy policies, linked above. We encourage you to read them.

No accounts, no tracking cookies, no analytics, no advertising

There is nothing to sign up for — Seasonist has no accounts and no login. It sets no tracking or advertising cookies and uses no cookie-based cross-site tracking, which is why you will not see a cookie consent banner: there is nothing to consent to. There are no analytics or measurement tools, no advertising or ad networks, no marketing, and no profiling. We do not build a profile of you and we do not sell, rent or share personal data — because we hold none.
